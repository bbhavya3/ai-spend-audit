# Go To Market Strategy

## Target User

The primary target users are:
- Startup founders
- Engineering managers
- Indie hackers
- Small AI startup teams

Especially companies spending heavily on AI subscriptions without proper cost optimization.

## Discovery Channels

Potential users may discover this tool through:
- Twitter/X startup discussions
- Reddit communities like r/startups and r/SideProject
- Product Hunt launches
- Hacker News
- Indie Hacker communities

## First 100 Users Strategy

To acquire the first 100 users:
- Post demo videos on Twitter/X
- Share screenshots on Reddit
- Launch on Product Hunt
- Reach out to startup founders directly
- Share the tool in developer Discord servers

## Unique Advantage

The main advantage is combining AI spend visibility with actionable savings recommendations. Most startups currently lack visibility into unnecessary AI expenses.

## Success Metrics

Week 1 success would look like:
- 100+ completed audits
- 20+ lead captures
- 5+ consultation requests
- Organic sharing on social platforms
