# Economics

## Lead Value

A converted startup customer could be worth approximately $500 to $5000 depending on the size of AI infrastructure purchases and recurring usage.

## Customer Acquisition Cost

Estimated CAC:
- Twitter/X organic posts: near $0
- Product Hunt launch: near $0
- Reddit promotion: near $0
- Founder outreach: time investment only

This creates an extremely low-cost acquisition model initially.

## Conversion Funnel

Estimated funnel:
- 100 audits completed
- 20 users save reports
- 5 users request consultation
- 1–2 customers purchase credits

Even low conversion rates could remain profitable because infrastructure deals have high value.

## Path to $1M ARR

To achieve $1M ARR:
- Acquire 200 paying companies
- Average yearly revenue per company: $5000
- Strong organic distribution and referral growth would be required

## Key Assumption

The biggest assumption is that startups are actively overspending on AI tooling and are motivated to optimize those costs quickly.
