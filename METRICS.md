# Metrics

## Key Product Metrics

### Audit Conversion Rate
Percentage of users who complete the audit form.

### Report Save Rate
Percentage of users who submit their email to save the report.

### Average Estimated Savings
Average projected monthly savings generated by the platform.

### Most Selected AI Tools
Tracks which AI tools users audit most frequently.

### Share Report Usage
Measures how often users open public shareable reports.

### Repeat Usage
Tracks users returning to run multiple audits.
